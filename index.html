Whats New

  • New School Canteen

    In recent years, we have been improving on St Xavier's canteen. We have increased the amount and variety of food served at the canteen to better suit our students. Online menus with pictures and descriptions of the food prepared help students and parents to better choose food according to nutritional value. This will help students avoid allergens in their food or help them follow any dietary restrictions. We will also make ordering online available so that it is easier for our students and staff alike.

  • Implemented Online Attendance Management

    Human beings are capable of making mistakes even if they are provided with devices. Because of this, St Xavier School has decided to implement online attendance management. This will save money and time, making a vast difference to the school management, while ensuring your child's safety.
